MENU PLAY

This game is based on obstacles from American Ninja Warrior. It involves controlling the player by having them run, jump from wall to wall over various obstacles. When the user engages with the arrow keys (LEFT | RIGHT | UP | DOWN), the player will move in the direction of the arrow key. The usersâ€™ high scores could be traced through the Firebase and their login information.

The game will have targets that requires the player to move as fast as possible to collect stars and each star will give a score of 10. The goal will be to get every star that appears. If you hit obstacles more than three times or you let a target time out, the game ends! The technology used in the development of the game includes Phaser.io, Express.js, Firebase, MySQL, MVC, ORM and User Authentication
